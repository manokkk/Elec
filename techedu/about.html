St. Theodore School, Inc. envisions itself as a dynamic learning institution, committed to quality education and services that develop basic physical, social, moral, and spiritual values producing the next generation of innovative, loving, and progressive students and teachers in an enriching, nurturing, and constantly changing environment who can introduce positive changes in the society.

To develop the well-being and potentials of students and teachers, St. Theodore School, Inc. provides enjoyable learning experiences that will instill in them the yearning to learn and the love for knowledge to become more responsible citizens in the community. To empower students and teachers to become leaders who are: equipped, flexible, and open to constant changes in the environment (especially in the different modes of learning) ready to give a genuine and selfless kind of service beyond what is expected. To achieve this purpose, other stakeholders like the parents, church workers, and other people in the community they meet along their journey are also given importance and considered as part of the support system to achieve the vision.
